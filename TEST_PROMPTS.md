# Test Prompts for Clinical Decision Making Application

## Purpose
These prompts demonstrate how this medical AI chatbox differs from a normal AI chatbox by:
- Providing medical-specific, accurate information
- Including safety disclaimers
- Focusing on clinical decision support
- Maintaining medical context throughout conversations

---

## Category 1: Symptom Analysis & Diagnosis Support

### Prompt 1: General Symptom Query
**Input:** "I've been experiencing persistent headaches for the past week, what could be causing this?"

**Expected Differences from Normal AI:**
- Provides medical context (tension, migraine, underlying conditions)
- Mentions when to seek immediate medical attention
- Includes disclaimer about consulting healthcare professionals
- Focuses on clinical decision-making factors

### Prompt 2: Multiple Symptoms
**Input:** "I have fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. What should I do?"

**Expected Differences:**
- Recognizes potential emergency symptoms
- Prioritizes urgent care recommendations
- Provides clear action steps
- Emphasizes immediate medical attention when needed

### Prompt 3: Chronic Symptom Pattern
**Input:** "I get chest pain after exercise that goes away with rest. Should I be concerned?"

**Expected Differences:**

## Key Differences from Normal AI Chatbox

### 1. Medical Accuracy
- ✅ Provides evidence-based information
- ✅ Uses medical terminology appropriately
- ✅ Focuses on clinical relevance
- ❌ Normal AI: May provide general information without medical context

### 2. Safety Focus
- ✅ Includes disclaimers about consulting healthcare professionals
- ✅ Recognizes emergency situations
- ✅ Emphasizes medical supervision
- ❌ Normal AI: May not emphasize medical consultation

### 3. Clinical Decision Support
- ✅ Helps with clinical decision-making
- ✅ Provides structured information
- ✅ Focuses on actionable medical guidance
- ❌ Normal AI: General conversation without clinical focus

### 4. Context Awareness
- ✅ Maintains medical context throughout
- ✅ Understands medical relationships
- ✅ Provides relevant clinical information
- ❌ Normal AI: May lose medical context

### 5. Professional Boundaries
- ✅ Acknowledges limitations
- ✅ Directs to appropriate care
- ✅ Maintains professional tone
- ❌ Normal AI: May overstep boundaries
